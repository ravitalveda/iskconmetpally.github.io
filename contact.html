Reaching us by bus:

From Hyderabad MGBS Bus Stand & Secundrabad Jubliee Bus Stand. There are Number of buses going to Metpally or Korutla.


By car:

On NH-44 from Hyderabad to Nizamabad Highway @ Armoor Junction 30km on Jagtial/Warangal route. The temple is located between Metpally and Korutla.


By train:

From Mumbai or Secunderabad you should arrive in Nizamabad. It is the nearest Railway Station to Metpally.

By air:

Hyderabad International airport is 279 km away from ISKCON Metpally. There are even direct busses available right from airport to Metpally.
